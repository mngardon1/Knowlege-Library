## The Client: Jesse

Jesse is an accomplished senior professional leaving corporate to build an independent consulting practice. He knows he can solve real problems for companies. He has the expertise. What he lacks is the structure to turn that expertise into a business.

**Where he is when he finds Michael:**
Already started. He's had some conversations, maybe landed one engagement through a referral. He's on LinkedIn, trying to get traction. But most conversations go nowhere. He's casting a wide net because he's afraid of leaving opportunity on the table — and that fear is exactly what's killing him.

**The real problem:**
He's not specific enough. Vague positioning produces vague conversations. Vague conversations don't close. Jesse is articulate, credible, and capable — but buyers can't see exactly what he does, for whom, and what they get. So they nod and move on.

**What Jesse feels:**
Anxious about income stability. Frustrated that his expertise isn't converting. Uncertain whether this is going to work. Underneath that: desperate for a system he can trust — something that tells him what to do next, not just principles.

---

## The Offer: The Upstream Consultant

A structured coaching program for post-corporate independent consultants who need to get specific, get visible, and build predictable revenue.

**The three-phase framework: Position → Package → Pipeline**

1. **Position** — Define exact value. Identify the specific problem you solve, for whom, and why you over anyone else. The Person/Problem/POV model.
2. **Package** — Build a repeatable, sellable offer. Buyers understand your wheelhouse immediately. No more wide nets.
3. **Pipeline** — Build a visible, manageable engine for predictable revenue. Confidence in prospecting conversations. Real income trajectory.

**The differentiator:**
Every phase is built with AI — not as a gimmick, but as a working system. Jesse leaves with an AI infrastructure that works alongside him in every facet of his business, so he's not stuck in perpetual biz dev mode. He can deliver and sell simultaneously.

## The Category Michael Is Building

**Positioning coaching for independent consultants** — specifically post-corporate professionals who have the expertise but not the business architecture.

This is not generic business coaching. It's not a "how to start consulting" course. It's a guided build of a specific, repeatable consulting business — with AI embedded from day one.

The adjacent but distinct framing: most consultants fail not from lack of skill but from lack of specificity. Michael's program solves the specificity problem at the root.
