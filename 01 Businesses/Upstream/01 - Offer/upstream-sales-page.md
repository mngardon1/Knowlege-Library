## How It's Built

### Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4
**Positioning: Your Market-Facing Foundation**

We close the gap between how you see your value and how your market sees it. Your offer gets sharpened to a specific problem you solve for a specific buyer. When positioning is tight, the right people recognize you immediately — and pre-sell themselves before they ever talk to you.

### Phase 2 — Weeks 5–8
**Package: Your Conversion Engine**

With positioning locked, we build the asset layer that does the selling for you. A landing page, VSL, or case study hub where the right buyer spends time with your thinking — and chooses to take action. This is where your positioning becomes a funnel. We connect it to a lightweight CRM so every lead is tracked and nothing falls through the cracks.

### Phase 3 — Weeks 9–12
**Pipeline: Getting Your Offer In Front of the Right People**

With your Package converting, we build everything that drives traffic to it — referral scripts, outreach sequences, content system, and distribution channels. Every asset is designed to put the right buyer in front of your Package consistently. Your AI agent produces marketing content from your positioning so the pipeline never goes quiet.

### Upstream O.S. — Ongoing
**Your Operating System: The Wrapper That Makes It Run**

Once you've done the hard work of solidifying your positioning, pipeline and conversion processes, we embed that context into your personal AI Operating System that allows you to systematize this work. This is not AI slop, but rather targeted outreach, content, scripts and proposals in your voice and your style. We have natural feedback loops that continue to mature as your consulting practice matures, ensuring it keeps compounding after this 90-day program. Maintenance: 2 hours a week.
