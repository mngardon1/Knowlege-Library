## Rating Criteria

Use these criteria to assign ratings. Be honest — a diagnostic that rates everything "DEVELOPING" is useless.

**STRONG** — Clear, specific, and differentiated. The person can articulate this in plain language. A buyer would immediately understand it. Sufficient raw material for the Deep Dive to build on. Ready for sharpening, not discovery.

**DEVELOPING** — Has raw material but needs focusing. They have instincts or examples but can't yet state it cleanly. The Deep Dive has something to work with but will need to push for specificity. 1-2 rounds of sharpening work needed.

**UNDEVELOPED** — Vague, generic, or missing. Uses language that could describe hundreds of other consultants. Or couldn't articulate it at all. The Deep Dive will need to do significant extraction work in this circle. Needs substantial development.

## Rules

- Always include at least 3 STRENGTH quotes and 3 GAP quotes
- Always include at least 2 BUYER LANGUAGE quotes (or note explicitly that none were captured)
- The "Key gap" under each circle should be specific and actionable, not generic
- The Cross-Circle Alignment section must be filled out honestly — this is critical input for the Deep Dive
- The Deep Dive Priorities must be ranked and specific — not "work on everything"
- Evidence Basis must be noted for each circle. Pre-launch clients will have "Projected" for most things. That's fine — just flag it so the Deep Dive knows what needs market validation.

## Deep Dive Priority Logic

- DEFAULT: If multiple circles are weak, prioritize PROBLEM first. Most consultants in this program come from deep domain expertise. They don't lack awareness of problems — they lack commitment to ONE problem sharp enough to position around. Nailing the problem makes Person obvious (who has this problem worst?) and POV natural (why does this problem persist?).
- Exception: If Problem is already STRONG but Person is UNDEVELOPED, prioritize Person.
- Exception: If both Problem and Person are STRONG but POV is weak, prioritize POV.
- If all three are STRONG with strong alignment, the Deep Dive can move directly to sharpening and synthesis.
- Cross-circle misalignment ALWAYS gets flagged as a priority, regardless of individual circle ratings.
- Always include the save/next-step message at the end.
