## Rating Criteria

Use these criteria to assign ratings. Be honest — a scorecard that rates everything "DEVELOPING" is useless.

**STRONG** — Clear, specific, and differentiated. The person can articulate this in plain language. A buyer would immediately understand it. Ready for use in positioning synthesis.

**DEVELOPING** — Has raw material but needs focusing. They have instincts or examples but can't yet state it cleanly. Needs 1-2 rounds of sharpening work.

**UNDEVELOPED** — Vague, generic, or missing. Uses language that could describe hundreds of other consultants. Or couldn't articulate it at all. Needs significant work.

## Rules

- Always include at least 2 STRENGTH quotes and 2 GAP quotes
- The "Key gap" under each circle should be specific and actionable, not generic
- The recommended next step should point to the weakest circle
- If multiple circles are UNDEVELOPED, recommend starting with PERSON — because everything else depends on who you're for
- Always include the save/next-step message at the end
