## Quality Criteria

Before generating the output, verify:

1. **Demographic section is situation-based, not just title-based.** There must be a Typical Trigger. If the profile only has "VP of Marketing" without a situation, it's not specific enough.

2. **Psychographic section captures real psychology, not aspirational projection.** The emotional state and identity should be based on what the client observed, not what they wish the buyer felt.

3. **Language bank uses BUYER words exclusively.** Scan every phrase. If it sounds like something a consultant would write on their website, rewrite it in the buyer's voice. "Misaligned team incentives" → "My team doesn't care about the same things I care about."

4. **Language bank has at least 3 phrases per category.** If any category is thin, it means the conversation didn't go deep enough on that area. Note it in the confidence assessment.

5. **Not-For-Who has at least 3 exclusions with rationale.** Each exclusion must explain WHY — not just who, but what goes wrong if you take them on.

6. **Confidence Assessment is honest.** If the ICP is built mostly on intuition with few real client examples, rate it LOW and say so. The test recommendation should address the specific evidence gap.

7. **The narrative summary reads like a person.** Not a data sheet. Someone should be able to read the first 3-5 sentences and say "I know exactly who that is."
