# The Upstream Diagnostic

**Upstream OS · Week 1 · Step 2**

---

> *You can't build sharp positioning on a blurry baseline. This is how we find out where you actually stand.*

---

## What This Is

Before we build anything, we need to know exactly where you stand.

The Upstream Diagnostic is a 25–30 minute conversation with the bot below. It assesses your current positioning across three dimensions — **Person, Problem, and POV** — and produces the baseline document that feeds everything in Phase 1.

Most consultants walk in thinking their positioning is "pretty good." The diagnostic usually disagrees. That's the point. You want to know the truth now, not six months from now.

---

## Come Prepared

You don't need to rehearse. But have these ready before you start:

- **Your best guesses** — the problem you think you solve and for whom. Rough is fine.
- **Your 2–3 best clients** — not your biggest, your best. What was happening in their world when they hired you?
- **Your current positioning language** — your LinkedIn headline, how you describe yourself at a dinner party. Give the real version, not a polished one.
- **Any existing docs** — website copy, offer documents, prior positioning work. Upload them when the bot asks. The more context it has, the sharper the output.

---

## What the Bot Will Do

It goes one question at a time. It will push you when your answers are vague — that's the point.

Answer honestly. Messy answers are more useful than polished ones.

At the end, it produces your `upstream-diagnostic.md` — a scored baseline across all three dimensions with gap analysis and a clear starting point for the Positioning Deep Dive in Phase 1.
