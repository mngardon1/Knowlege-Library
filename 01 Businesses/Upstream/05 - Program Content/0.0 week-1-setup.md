# Week 1: Setup

**Upstream OS · Phase 1 · Before We Build Anything**

---

> *Most consultants fail at positioning before they start. They skip the setup and wonder why nothing sticks. This week closes that gap.*

---

## What This Week Is For

This week has one job: get you ready to do the real work.

Not mindset content. Not theory. Three specific things that have to exist before Phase 1 can produce anything useful. Skip any one of them and the work that follows will be slower, messier, and harder to build on.

By the end of this week you will have:

- A knowledge system ready to receive every artifact you build over the next 90 days
- A clear, scored diagnostic of where your positioning actually stands right now
- The right psychological frame for what comes next — so you don't quietly sabotage the work before it begins

**Estimated time to complete: 2.5 hours**

---

## ⚠️ Do These In Order

The sequence matters. Don't run the diagnostic before your system is set up. Don't touch positioning before you've done the reset. Each step is designed to feed the next.

---

## This Week's Steps

---

### Step 1 — Set Up Your Upstream O.S.
**⏱ 10 minutes**

Before you build anything, you need a place to put it.

The Upstream O.S. is the folder structure that holds every artifact you'll create over the next 90 days — your positioning brief, your ICP profiles, your offers, your content. Without it, the work scatters. With it, everything has a home and a version history.

This takes ten minutes. Do it now, before anything else.

**→ Go to: Set Up Your Upstream O.S.**

---

### Step 2 — Run the Upstream Diagnostic
**⏱ 15–20 minutes**

Before you start building new positioning, you need to know exactly what's broken about your current one.

The Positioning Scorecard Bot runs you through a structured diagnostic across three dimensions — **Person, Problem, and POV** — and scores each one. You'll leave with a clear picture of where your positioning is strong, where it's weak, and where to focus first.

This is your first artifact. Save the output directly into your Upstream O.S.

**→ Go to: The Upstream Diagnostic**

---

### Step 3 — The Psychological Reset
**⏱ 20–30 minutes**

One short read before you touch positioning.

Most consultants walk into this phase carrying the wrong frame — about what positioning is, what it's supposed to feel like, and what "getting it right" actually means. That frame quietly derails the work. It creates perfectionism where there should be experimentation. It creates attachment where there should be curiosity.

This reset clears it. It's also your bridge into Phase 1.

**→ Go to: The Positioning Psychology Reset**

---

You're ready to move to Phase 1 when:

- [ ] Your Upstream O.S. folder structure is built
- [ ] Your Diagnostic output is saved as your first artifact
- [ ] You've completed the Psychology Reset

All three checked? You're ready.
