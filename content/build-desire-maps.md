# Desire Maps — Build

Desire Maps describe the felt-state outcomes that emerge once Build is complete.
They reflect sustainable execution, healthy risk orientation, and value clarity while acting under uncertainty.

---

## Desire Map D1 — Clean Action & Creative Momentum
Action feels necessary, right, and creatively alive.

Taking action feels true, playful, and intrinsically motivating.
Execution is experienced as creative expression rather than obligation.

---

## Desire Map D2 — Fluid Relationship with Money
Money feels creatable, dynamic, and responsive.

Income is no longer psychologically tethered to a job.
Money is treated as feedback and constraint, not threat.

---

## Desire Map D3 — Informational Relationship with Uncertainty
Uncertainty feels neutral-to-energizing.

Uncertainty is treated as information about what to try next.
Possibility thinking replaces threat-based thinking.

---

## Desire Map D4 — Accelerated Learning & Empowered Growth
Growth feels empowering instead of chaotic.

Learning accelerates; patterns become visible; judgment improves.
Confidence is rooted in experience rather than guessing.

---

## Desire Map D5 — Exit Readiness & Value Clarity
Leaving corporate feels obvious, not dramatic.

Clarity exists on:
- who they want to help
- how their value can be used
- what problems they are best suited to solve

Staying feels optional; leaving feels earned.
