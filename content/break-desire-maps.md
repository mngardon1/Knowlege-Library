# Desire Maps — Break

Desire Maps describe the felt-state outcomes that emerge once Break is complete.
They are stable internal experiences, not goals or behaviors.

---

## Desire Map B1 — Time Sovereignty
Time feels owned, not borrowed.

A person experiences full psychological ownership over their time and consciously allocates hours instead of reacting to demands.
Different hours hold different value, creating freedom to invest time into:
- future thinking
- health
- relationships
- curiosity
- play

---

## Desire Map B2 — Instrumentalized Employment
The job feels like a tool, not a master.

The person experiences their job as working for them instead of the other way around.
They focus on the 20% that drives real performance and ignore the 80% of distracting, low-value activity.
The job becomes a partner and an investor in their future.

---

## Desire Map B3 — Recovered Energy & Momentum
Energy returns naturally without force.

Once non-serving activities are removed, energy and motivation rise.
People often describe surprise at how much time and energy they reclaimed.
This restored energy expresses as improved mood, motivation, happiness, and forward momentum.
