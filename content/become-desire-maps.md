# Desire Maps — Become

Desire Maps describe the felt-state outcomes that emerge once Become is complete.
They reflect identity stability, embodied desire, and future-self integration.

---

## Desire Map C1 — Identity Calm & Internal Control
A deep sense of calm paired with internal control.

The person experiences emotional steadiness even under pressure.
Worries arise but no longer consume attention or dictate behavior.
Blockers feel solvable; capability feels available and grounded.

---

## Desire Map C2 — Embodied Desire & Moral Permission
Desire feels clean, allowed, and directional.

True motivations are clear at a visceral level and become priorities.
Wanting more no longer feels selfish — it feels expansive.
They are comfortable wanting differently than the crowd.

---

## Desire Map C3 — Integrated Relationship with the Past
The past is held with kindness instead of judgment.

Past choices are seen as reasonable decisions made with limited information.
They keep what is useful, release what no longer serves, and view the past as necessary investment.

---

## Desire Map C4 — Future-Self Pull & Behavioral Standard
The future self acts as a pull, not a pressure.

The future identity is a living reference point that sets standards now.
Decisions are guided by: “What would my future self do here?”

---

## Desire Map C5 — Identity Resonance & Natural Courage
Action feels called for, not forced.

The future identity is emotionally resonant and self-recognizable.
Action feels aligned and internally compelled rather than scary.
